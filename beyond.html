Beyond Work

Things that shaped me outside a job title — leadership, teaching, community, and small rituals that mattered.

SWE — Society of Women Engineers

Roles: President (2025–2026), Treasurer (2023–May 2025)

  • Organized and hosted service & social events to build community and mentorship.
  • Reduced club spending by ≈ $450 per semester through better budgeting & vendor negotiation.
  • Planned a fundraiser for BrightLane Learning and collected $150+ in donations.
  • Ran outreach introducing K-12 students to coding (Scratch) at a Girl Scouts event (2024).

Mentoring & Tutoring

I enjoy helping others get started and stay in engineering.

  • Peer mentoring: guided ~10 new engineering students (2024–2025).
  • Teaching assistant / tutor at R.B. Annis School of Engineering — supported Computer Architecture and Intro Engineering courses (Aug 2024 – present).
  • Hosted office hours and small-group workshops to explain assembly concepts and basic programming patterns.

Clubs & Community Nights

  • Secretary, MSA (Muslim Student Association) — organized community meetups and cultural events.
  • Racing Club — recent member; helped with event planning and track days.
  • Hosted weekly game nights — casual community-building sessions focusing on board/digital games and group projects.

iD Tech — Summer Instructor (Summer 2025)

Instructor for BattleBots Robotics (VEX / C++) and Minecraft Modding (Java). Taught students (ages 9–17) hardware, programming, and teamwork.

  • Taught robotics programming (C++), led design & testing cycles, and coached teams through competitions.
  • Delivered Minecraft Modding lessons with Forge — students built custom items and mods.
  • Maintained a safe & inclusive learning environment while adapting lessons for varied skill levels.

Outreach & Volunteering

  • Introduced K-12 students to programming and robotics through local events and workshops.
  • Organized fundraising and service events for campus initiatives and partner nonprofits.
  • Volunteer mentor for community tech literacy projects.
